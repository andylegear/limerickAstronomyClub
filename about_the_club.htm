The Limerick Astronomy Club (LAC) was founded on February 6th, 1986. From the mid-1990s until 2018, the club was known as the Shannonside Astronomy Club (SAC), but we eventually decided to revert to our original name.

LAC is composed of members who share a common interest in the various aspects of astronomy from Limerick, Clare, Tipperary and further afield. We were a founding member of IFAS (The Irish Federation of Astronomical Societies).

As part of our commitment to bringing astronomy to the public in the region, we provide talks for schools and arrange occasional public gatherings for major celestial events.

We run a bulletin board for people to discuss astronomical related subjects - it's a friendly place to chat with other amateur astronomers of all levels.
